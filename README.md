👨‍💼 Employee Exit Insights Dashboard
📌 Project Story

This dashboard analyzes employee exit data to understand workforce trends,
identify reasons for employee turnover, and help management improve retention strategies. 
The goal is to provide clear insights into why employees leave and which departments are most affected.

At a high level, the dashboard shows:

Total Exits: 38
Resignations: 30
Terminations: 2
Death in Service: 3

These KPIs give a quick overview of overall employee attrition in the company.

📊 Key Analysis

The dashboard provides insights into different areas:

Department-wise Exits:
Certain departments like Field Sales and DMS have higher exit counts, indicating possible workload 
or management challenges.
Exit Reason Distribution:
Resignation is the most common reason for employee exits, while termination and other reasons are minimal.
Monthly Exit Trends:
Employee exits vary over time, with some months showing higher exits, which may indicate seasonal or internal factors.
Exit Trends by Type:
Resignations dominate across all periods, showing a consistent pattern in employee behavior.
💡 Key Insights
Majority of employees leave through resignation
A few departments contribute to most exits
Exit trends fluctuate across months
Other exit types are very low compared to resignations
✅ Conclusion

This dashboard helps organizations understand employee attrition patterns and identify key problem areas.
By analyzing exit reasons and department performance, management can take proactive steps 
to improve employee satisfaction, reduce turnover, and build a more stable workforce.
